Given the bind for farmers between the pandemic and climate change the Missouri Farm Bureau suggested farmers to look for other methods of income, specifically by using renewable energy on their land.

In my Audiences & Persuasion class at the University of Missouri, my group and I were tasked with developing a recommendation on a messaging strategy to persuade farmers to convert some of their acreage to renewable engery.
