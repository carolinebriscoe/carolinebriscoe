Throughout this course, I was placed with a group of students from an array of disciplinary backgrounds to develop a potential solution to the United Nations' SDG 2: Zero Hunger. We spent a semester working through a series of modules touching on numerous SDGs in order to use the information to propose a relevant solution to world hunger.

The Details

Our country: Haiti

Project name: Port-Au-Prosper

Issue at hand: recurring natural disasters

Our proposal: Port-au-Prosper is dedicated to partnering with local initiatives to ensure transparency of fund allocation through an accessible database!
